Privacy Policy

Overview
ANONAMAIL.ORG is a privacy-focused email service. Our mail servers are located in the Netherlands.

Data Collection
We minimize data collection. We do not store identifiable personal information beyond what is absolutely necessary to provide service. Account sign-up is handled via our Telegram bot (@bot_anonamailorg) where you must state the purpose for using the service.

Encryption
All stored mail and transport are encrypted. We do not read user emails and we do not sell user data.

Retention & Logs
We keep minimal logs required for operation and security. Identifiable metadata is not retained in a way that can be linked to individuals.

Abuse & Safety
If you observe abuse or illegal use of the service, report it to abuse@anonamail.org, childsafety@anonamail.org or csam@anonamail.org.

Contact
For questions about privacy, contact abuse@anonamail.org.
