Private, Encrypted Email - Based in the Netherlands

ANONAMAIL is a privacy-first email service designed for users who prioritize confidentiality and control over their communications. We combine strong cryptographic protections, limited data retention policies, and transparent operational practices to offer an email service that minimizes exposure of personal information while remaining reliable and easy to use.

What We Protect

We focus on protecting the content of your messages and the minimal metadata necessary to deliver mail. Our protections include:

  • End-to-end encryption: Messages are encrypted so only intended recipients can read them where feasible.
  • Minimal logging: We retain the smallest amount of metadata required for service operation and abuse handling.
  • Data residency: All primary servers and backups are hosted in the Netherlands to benefit from strong legal safeguards for user privacy.

How to Register

To request an account, contact our registration bot on Telegram: @bot_anonamailorg. In your message, briefly state the intended use of the account (personal, business, project, etc.). We review requests to ensure responsible use of the service and to protect the safety of our users. Typical approval time is 24–72 hours depending on volume.

Usage Guidelines

ANONAMAIL is intended for lawful communications. To keep the service secure and accessible for everyone, please adhere to the following:

  • Do not use the service for activities that facilitate harm or illegal conduct.
  • Respect intellectual property and do not distribute copyrighted material without permission.
  • Keep account credentials confidential and enable any offered account security features.

Reporting Abuse and Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behaviour that violates our policies or poses risk to others, please report it so our Trust & Safety team can investigate. Use the appropriate email address below depending on the nature of the report:

  • General abuse reports: abuse@anonamail.org
  • Child safety / CSAM concerns: childsafety@anonamail.org and csam@anonamail.org

Please include the following when reporting:

  1. Clear description of the issue and why it is abusive or harmful.
  2. Relevant headers, message timestamps, and message IDs or URLs when available.
  3. Any steps you have already taken (blocked user, preserved evidence, etc.).

We triage reports and aim to acknowledge receipt within 48 hours. Complex investigations may require additional time; please remain available for follow-up questions from our team. For immediate danger or threats of physical harm, contact local authorities first.

Transparency and Accountability

We are committed to transparency about how we handle user data and abuse reports. Our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use detail the legal framework and procedures we follow. We will notify affected users when action is taken that directly impacts their account, except where notification would jeopardize an investigation or safety.

Support and Response Expectations

Our Support Team handles general account questions, access issues, and technical assistance. Typical response windows are:

  • Account requests and general support: 24–72 hours
  • Abuse acknowledgements: within 48 hours
  • Investigations: variable, depending on complexity

If your support request is time-sensitive, mark the message clearly and provide a concise explanation of the urgency. We will prioritize genuinely urgent requests where possible.

Legal and Law Enforcement Requests

We respond to lawful requests from authorities according to applicable law. When possible and permitted, we notify affected users about such requests. Our legal team evaluates each request to ensure it meets statutory standards before any disclosure is made.
